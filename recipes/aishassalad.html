Aisha's Salad

Description

Once the days start warming up but they've still got that pleasent breeze in the air, (think April and May) I excitedly wait by the phone for my best friend Aisha to call.
She has hosted some of the best parties I have ever been to. In fact, the setting of all my favourite memories is the terrace of her gorgeous London townhouse.

This deliciously mouth watering salad is a part of one such memory. I still remember the frantic phone call I had with her 20 minutes before her party (which was being held for me) started.
"Anandita PLEASE! please. PLEASE! I know it's your birthday but I NEED you to come help me. I'm a mess!." she begged in her Italian accent.

I didn't think much of this salad at first - after all, it was a product of last minute decisions. But, for some peculiar reason, I just couldn't get it out of my mind.
This salad is so special to Aisha and I that it almost feels like I am betraying her by showing it to the world. However, she and I both agree that it is too good to not tell you all about it.

So, with that said, here is what you'll need to prepare it.

Ingredients

Salad:

  • Rocket
  • Parsely
  • Mint
  • Walnuts
  • Feta

Dressing:
