Aisha's Salad

Description

No, that isn't a typo. This is Kay's Lime pie, not key lime pie.
Well, technically, this is a key lime pie recipe, but I was thought it by my aunt Kay.
She is one of the best bakers in the world. I'm not being hyperbolic. In fact, once I'm more intermediate with my coding skills, you best believe I'm going to make her a top class website so that she can sell her baked treats online. The story behind this cake goes like this: I had just gone through my first break up (I had just been informed that Justin Bieber was in a serious relationship with Selena Gomez. I. was. disraught) and to console me, she baked this wonderful treat. It was the first time I'd ever tasted key lime pie; since then, I have never eaten anyone else's key lime pie and I do not wish to.
So, if you're a key lime pie virgin, I highly encourage you to lose that virginity to this recipe.

Ingredients

For the crust:

  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 1/4 graham cracker crushed

For the filling:

  • 4 egg yolks
  • 14 ounces sweetened condensed milk
  • 6 key limes for zesting and juicing
  • Whipped topping for garnish (optional)
  • Feta
